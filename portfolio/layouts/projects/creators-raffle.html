Creator's Raffle Movie Poster

“Creator’s Raffle” is a made-up movie where a shipyard worker is told he’s won the opportunity to become a “Creator,” in which a random person from an eligible universe gets the chance to create their own universe. This movie would fall into categories that other stories such as “A Wrinkle in Time” and Studio Ghibli Productions would. The beautiful surrealism of giant glowing goldfish swimming through the sky, as well as the humble environment of a shipyard combine to communicate the feeling of wonder in an average and unassuming place. Aiding in that, the 1-point perspective creates a tunnel for the viewers eyes to delve into which mimic the all enveloping awe the character in the scene would be feeling.
